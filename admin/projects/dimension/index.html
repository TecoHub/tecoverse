Intro

I'm Mehdi, Unique name? Ordering Starbucks I usually use Mike. I'm a frontend developer based in Japan, and I specialize in efficient React Apps and CSS & HTML that just work accors all platforms and browsers. I care deeply about building interfaces that are usable and pleasant for the most number of people possible.

My professional life has been 100% driven by my passion and Computers have been a life-long fascination for me. What started as a simple interest in using them, developed into a growing desire to understand how a collection of simple ideas and components can interact to produce an entity as complex and adaptable as a computer. I like to keep up to date with science and technology news and I am aregular reader of ‘The New Scientist’.

Right now, I’m excited about the still very complicated WebAssembly, I also plan to explore the “server-side” more and become a better-rounded full-stack dev.
